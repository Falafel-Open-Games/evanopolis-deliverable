# EVANOPOLIS Background Asset Design Guide

last update: 2026-04-27

## Overview
This document defines the prompts and design rules for generating six consistent background illustrations for EVANOPOLIS.  
These are **UI background assets**, not concept art. They must remain clean, simple, and consistent to support logos and UI overlays.

---

## 🎨 Global Style Lock

**STRICT VECTOR MODE**
- Flat vector illustration ONLY
- No texture, grain, or noise
- No painterly or concept-art rendering
- No lighting realism

**SHAPE RULES**
- Large, simple geometric shapes
- Minimal detail
- No micro elements
- Simplify all objects to basic forms

**COLOR RULES**
- Slightly desaturated palette
- Soft gradients only
- No harsh contrast
- No deep shadows

**COMPOSITION RULES**
- Wide 16:9
- Clear empty space in the center for UI overlay
- Elements placed to left and right
- Clean layered depth (foreground / midground / background)

**CONSISTENCY**
- All 6 images must look like they were made by the same artist
- Same visual language, same level of simplification

## 📋 Individual Prompts

### Generate Ciudad del Este
**Scene:**
- Hydroelectric dam on the left  
- Wide river flowing through center  
- Modern bridge crossing river  
- Containers and trucks on the right  
- Small city skyline in distance  
- Green hills and vegetation  

**Composition:**
- Keep center open (river/sky)  
- Elements on sides  
- Balanced and calm  

**Style:**
- Follow GLOBAL STYLE LOCK strictly  
- No text, no logos  

---

### Generate Irkutsk
**Scene:**
- Frozen river with large ice sheets and cracks  
- Small flowing water channels  
- Hydro dam partially covered in snow  
- Sparse pine trees  
- Compact city near river  
- Snow terrain with exposed rock  

**Composition:**
- Large empty center (river or sky)  
- Emphasize scale and emptiness  
- Minimal elements  

**Style:**
- Follow GLOBAL STYLE LOCK strictly  
- No text, no logos  

---

### Generate Angra dos Reis
**Scene:**
- Nuclear power plant with two large cooling towers  
- Subtle steam rising from towers (soft gradient)  
- Coastal bay with calm water in foreground  
- Green hills and tropical vegetation around plant  
- Small town or port in distance  
- Simple road or bridge connecting to plant  

**Composition:**
- Wide 16:9  
- Open center (sky/water) for UI overlay  
- Cooling towers slightly off‑center, balanced by vegetation and coastline  
- Clean layered depth (foreground water, midground plant, background hills)  

**Style:**
- Follow GLOBAL STYLE LOCK strictly  
- No text, no logos
