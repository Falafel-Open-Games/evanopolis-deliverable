Use this unchanged in every generation:

A low-poly 3D modular diorama tile with a clean octagonal base.

Base:
A precise hard-surface regular octagonal tile with eight clearly defined flat sides.

Each side is a straight segment of equal length.
All eight corners are sharp and visible.
The silhouette must read as a clean polygon, not circular.

The top terrain strictly follows the octagonal boundary with no rounding.

The base has three aligned layers:
- flat top terrain layer
- thin middle accent strip following the same eight sides
- solid bottom block with vertical flat faces

No smoothing, no curvature, no circular shapes.

Style:
- minimal low-poly geometry
- clean shapes, no small details
- no textures, only flat colors with subtle gradients
- neutral daylight lighting
- soft shadows
- everything grounded, no floating geometry

Composition:
- slightly asymmetrical
- main subject is slightly off-center
- clear silhouette, easy to read
- designed as a modular tile
- the base resembles a board game tile with hard polygonal edges
- clearly visible eight-sided geometry

Constraints:
- no people, no vehicles, no roads
- no text or logos
- no clutter or micro details
- one strong hero element
- avoid circular or rounded base shapes
- avoid smooth or organic base outlines
- emphasize straight edges and flat polygonal surfaces
- show at least five distinct edges of the octagon in perspective

Camera:
- three-quarter view
- centered framing
- white background
